Cockatiel

The Cockatiel is a small, social parrot with distinctive feather crest on its head. They are known for their playful and affectionate nature, making them popular pets.

Cockatiel Favorite Things in Order!

  1. Whistling and singing
  2. Interacting with their owners
  3. Chewing and shredding toys
